## Notes for the fix (per-rule guidance)

- `no-console` (test reporters `flaky-reporter.ts`, `quarantine.ts`): reporters legitimately print â†’ narrow `// eslint-disable-next-line no-console` or a `packages/test-utils/src/reporters/**` config exemption (reporters-may-console is a valid policy).
- `@typescript-eslint/no-require-imports` (`AggregateExecutor.ts`): `require("../SolutionMapping")` is a **deliberate lazy-require breaking a circular dependency** â€” works at runtime. Fix needs the circular dep resolved OR a justified scoped disable; do NOT naively convert to static `import` (may reintroduce the cycle).
- `no-case-declarations` (`AlgebraSerializer.ts`): wrap `case` bodies in `{}` (mechanical, behavior-preserving).
- `@typescript-eslint/restrict-template-expressions`: wrap non-string `${x}` in `String(x)`.
