# req__Requirement

A single verifiable **functional** requirement of Exocortex, expressed as Gherkin Given/When/Then in the asset body (`req__Requirement_statement`). Bound to already-existing real tests via a `@req:<uid>` token in the test title (`_verifiedBy`, derived), valid only with revert-verify evidence (RFC 0003 §3.6). Architectural / non-functional requirements are **not** modelled here — they live as `.archgate` ADRs (RFC 0003 §3.9).

`req__Requirement ⊑ exo__Asset`.
